travel differently about

Take a Tour is an emerging prospective travel guide that delivers a cultural experience for its customers through a different scope. Our mission is to be the bridge between the locals and foreigners. We deliver authentic cultural experiences from food tasting to peculiar exciting affairs, we value adventure.

Our Goal:

Our plan is to be the founding platform that empowers small businesses in local regions to indulge to their fullest potential. Our starting point is to alleviate some of the problems found in the Human Development Goals that all tend to link back to finances and accessibilities. With the right promotion and deliverance, we believe these businesses will flourish through Take a Trip as we are the connectivity in bringing customers and investors directly to businesses while still finding a source of entertainment in whatever shape or form.

What we believe in:

As for what we value in regard to the targeted small businesses, Take a Trip values authenticity, Culture and Creativity. We accept hardworking individuals who see value and express a vision. We encourage small businesses to express how take a trip could potentially boost their potential and alleviate some of their conflicts.

In addition, we differentiate ourselves from other travel guides through our unique visionary scope. We believe that the essence of experience is truly found in a foreign country only once one integrates themselves with a different culture and directly engages with locals causing them to soak in a different lifestyle, and open horizons on a different perception of the world.
